About GreenWave

Your Premier Eco-Friendly Packaging Exporter

Our History

Founded in the heart of Bangladesh, the world's leading producer of high-quality jute, GreenWave was born from a desire to share this "golden fiber" with the world. We started as a small trading company with a big vision: to become a leading jute bag exporter from Bangladesh, known for reliability, quality, and a commitment to sustainability.

Over the years, we have grown our operations, building strong relationships with the best jute mills and developing a robust global logistics network. Today, we proudly serve a diverse range of clients across continents, from agricultural giants to fashion brands.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainable packaging worldwide by providing high-quality, affordable, and eco-friendly jute products. We aim to help businesses reduce their carbon footprint while offering a superior packaging alternative that is both durable and biodegradable.

Our Operational Excellence

Direct Mill Sourcing

We source directly from top-tier jute mills, eliminating middlemen. This ensures consistent quality, competitive pricing, and a transparent supply chain for our clients.

Own CNF & CIF Shipping

We manage our own Clearing and Forwarding (CNF) and offer seamless Cost, Insurance, and Freight (CIF) shipping. This control allows us to provide reliable, on-time delivery to any port worldwide.

Custom Jute Bag Manufacturer

As a custom jute bag manufacturer, we can create products tailored to your exact specifications, including size, weight, and custom printing for your brand.
