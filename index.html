Our Core Products

We offer a wide range of jute bags suitable for various industries. Buy jute bags in bulk direct from the source.

Binola Jute Bags

Durable and versatile bags, perfect for agricultural products like grains, potatoes, and onions. Known for their strength.

B-Twill Jute Bags

Heavy-duty bags commonly used for packing sugar, rice, and wheat. B-Twill offers excellent durability for bulk transport.

L.CEES Jute Bags

Lightweight and economical bags ideal for various packaging needs. A great eco-friendly alternative to plastic bags.

Hessian Jute Bags

Also known as burlap bags, these are perfect for coffee beans, nuts, and other goods. Breathable and moisture-absorbent.
